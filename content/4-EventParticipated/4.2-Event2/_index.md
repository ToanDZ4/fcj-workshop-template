# Summary: AWS Study Group – Community Tech Sharing

### Event Objectives
- Share real-world experience building and operating applications on AWS.
- Discuss best practices for serverless and container workloads.
- Give the community a chance to network and exchange ideas.

### Main Content
- **Serverless in practice:** when to choose Lambda + API Gateway + DynamoDB, and common pitfalls (cold starts, timeouts, cost).
- **Containers on AWS:** ECS vs EKS, and why Fargate simplifies operations.
- **Cost optimization:** right-sizing, using the Free Tier wisely, tagging, and cleaning up unused resources.
- **Security basics:** least-privilege IAM, protecting secrets with SSM Parameter Store.
- **Q&A and networking** with speakers and other attendees.

### What I Gained
- Reinforced the serverless and container patterns I had been learning and applying in my capstone.
- Practical cost-control habits that helped me stay within the Free Tier during my labs.
- New connections in the AWS community and useful follow-up resources.
- More confidence to share my own project and ask questions publicly.
